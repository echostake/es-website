What is ECHO?

ECHO, a privacy-focused universal coin with instant, direct and private transactions for everyone.

GPU efficient mining algorithm

The new ProgPoW algorithm enables GPU mining of maximum efficiency, making it economically impractical to upgrade to ASICs

Truly decentralized

ASIC resistant ProgPoW empowers fair coin distribution, without being consolidated by the government or huge corporations.

Privacy-focused

Echostake's superior anonymity improves upon Monero's RingCT technology as well as Zcoin's exposing balances

Instant transactions

A new block is confirmed approx. every 60 seconds providing throughput of XXX tx per minute

Transparent rules and operations

All code is open-source and is available on Github for review and audit. Support is available through email and discord.

Progressive rewards

Receive protocol rewards through a variety of methods: PoW, PoS, as well as Masternode rewards.
